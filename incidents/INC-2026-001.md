# INC-2026-001: Database Connection Pool Exhaustion

Date: 2026-03-19
Severity: P1
Duration: 33 minutes (15:42 - 16:15)

## What Happened
Traffic spike from marketing campaign caused DB connection
pool to saturate at limit of 10 connections.
/api/orders returned 500 errors for 33 minutes.

## Resolution
Increased connection pool size from 10 to 50.
Added connection timeout handling.
Optimized slow queries causing long-held connections.

## Status: Resolved
