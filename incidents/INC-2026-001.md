# INC-2026-001: Database Connection Pool Exhaustion

**Date:** 2026-03-19  
**Severity:** P1  
**Duration:** 33 minutes (15:42 — 16:15 UTC)  
**Status:** Resolved  

## What Happened

A traffic spike from a marketing campaign caused the database connection
pool to saturate at its limit of 10 connections. The `/api/orders` endpoint
returned 500 errors for 33 minutes until a hotfix was deployed.

## Resolution

Increased connection pool size from 10 to 50.  
Added connection timeout handling in `middleware/db_middleware.py`.  
Optimized slow queries causing long-held connections (`queries/orders.sql`).
